Lex Equipe is an Indian based secretarial and legal firm established to provide superior quality, knowledge based, effective, business oriented corporate legal services to its clients. Founded on best international practices, coupled with strong capabilities and experience of its members, Lex Equipe provides comprehensive corporate legal services and solutions to a broad spectrum of domestic and foreign clients, ranging from start-up companies to multinational corporations.
